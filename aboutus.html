about us

A pilgrimage is a journey or search of moral and spiritual significance. India is covered by many holy sites and with sacred rivers. Going on a pilgrimage is considered as a good Karma. Our motto is to take people to these holy places across India in the most convenient way and with utmost care.

We organize spiritual tours across India. Our packages are framed on the basis of weather conditions of the places. We avoid night journey by road for the convenience of our customers. We ensure comfortable accommodation, safe journey and good vegetarian food (preferably south Indian food in northern states). We also make the tour enjoyable by sharing most interesting facts and importance of the places. Bookings can be made online or by visiting our office.

frequently asked questions

What ID proof do I need to carry?

Any govt. proof(Pan card, Voter id, Driving license, Passport, Aadhar card). For senior citizens aadhar card is mandatory.

What if I forget to carry ID proof?

Travel without ID Proof is considered as ticket less travel as per railway rules. The company is not responsible for any action that would be taken by railway authorities in such cases. For flight travel, passengers would not be allowed inside the airport premises without the proper identification proof. Under the above circumstances the passenger is not eligible for any refund as per Railway/Airline regulations. Cancellation charges for return travel would be charged as per Railway/Airline regulation. Additionally, 25% of the remaining tour package cost, will be charged as cancellation by the travels. Remaining tour package amount will be refunded to the customer within seven working days. In this case the customer needs to share their account details.

What are the different modes of payment?

The individual can pay the tour package preferably through internet banking or by cash.

How do I know if my booking was successful?

After the payment has been made, within a working day the confirmation mail would be sent to the customers.

How can I cancel my ticket?

As per our norms, customer should give in writing for the cancellation of the trip.

What are the cancellation charges applicable?

For train/flight ticket, cancellation charges are collected as per the railways/airlines regulations. For hotels, cab bookings and food arrangements, cancellation charges would be charged as per respective operators. A cancellation fee of Rs.500 would be additionally charged by Sundaramani travels.

How do I get refund for tickets?

After cancellation, the cancellation charges as mentioned above will be deducted and the balance amount will be credited in the customer account within seven working days. Customer should share the bank account details for refund process. In cases where refund is made in the form of vouchers or coupons by third party operators, the same shall be obtained in the name of the customer. Please note that the company cannot provide cash for the equivalent voucher/coupon values.
